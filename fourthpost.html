Education inequality and income inequailty

Explaining how difference in education affects income disparity

Posted by John Johnson on April 28, 2023

The disparity between education quality and access had grown ever larger since the mid 1970’s. Earnings, wages, individual incomes, and household incomes have also become increasingly unequal. This inequality is sometimes attributed to whether or not someone has a college education, or the education level of one's parents

Everytime you want to create a new paragraph, use these paragraph containers. For concision, you should have no more than three paragraphs and a minimum of one.

Be sure every paragraph has a main claim or takes the reader somewhere new. Then unpack that claim. What is significant? What do you want your viewer to remember? What is at stake?

If you do not want another paragraph, then simply delete ones that are unecessary.

Please be sure each post has some closure. That is, the last paragraph should be memorable. If they remember nothing else from this page, what do you hope your viewer learned?
